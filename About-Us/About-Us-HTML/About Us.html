Kopa Booka

Goal

The goal was to make life easier for students of the university of Johannesburg by creating a digital platform that provides an opportunity for students to advertise textbooks that they wish to sell and also provides a bridge of communication between them and their interested buyers. Kopa Booka was developed by Monoametsi Mosemeng who is a University of Johannesburg student that studies BCOM General.
